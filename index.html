How to play:

  • 1. Upload an image, it will be resized and cut into tiles
  • 2. Slide the tiles by clicking a tile adjacent to the blank tile.
  • 3. Recreate your original image with the blank tile at the bottom right of the board.
  • 4. Try get the fastest time you can! Good luck.
